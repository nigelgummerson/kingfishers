Itinerary

27 June 1993 Left Gatwick, U.K.


New Zealand

29 June Arrived Auckland, New Zealand. Travelled to University of Auckland Field Station at Leigh.
2 July Returned to Auckland.
5 July Left Auckland, arrived Cairns, Australia.


Papua New Guinea

6 July Left Cairns, arrived Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea.
14 July Left Port Moresby, arrived Hoskins, New Britain. Truck to Bialla.


Into the Nakanais

17 July Driven to Kamatami.
18 July Trekked to Lele.
19 July Trekked to Muela.
20 July Trekked to Mile.
21 July Trekked to Mukul, and back to Mile.
22 July Trekked to Muela.
23 July Trekked to Kamatami and driven back to Bialla.


Bialla

25 July Trekked to Lake Hargy.


Lake Hargy

31 July Returned to Bialla.
1 August Left Bialla on M. V. Kondor (boat).
2 August Arrived in Rabaul.
4 August Driven to Wild Dog Gold Mine, the Bainings.


Wild Dog

13 August Left Rabaul on M. V. Kris (boat).
14 August Arrived Kimbe.
20 AugustFlew to Cape Gloucester. Taken to Laut Village.
21 August Walked to Hornbill Point.


Hornbill Point

26 August Driven to Aimola village.
27 August Trekked to riverine camp site. Mount Talawe
3 September Returned to Cape Gloucester Station.
4 September Sailed to Kimbe on Kimbe Express.


Homeward Bound

7 September Returned to Port Moresby.
12 September Left Papua New Guinea, arrived Cairns.
14 September Left Cairns, arrived Auckland.
15 September Left Auckland.
16 September Arrived Home.
