About Nakanai '93

(Including a few words about these pages)

Nakania 93 involved four Oxford students who visited New Britain Island, Papua New Guinea and studied the birdlife out there. We had an excellent time and were made very welcome during our stay.

You can find loads of cool photos (all thumbnails with links to bigger photos) here:

  • People and Places 1
  • People and Places 2
  • Bird Photos
These are probably the best place to go if you're just surfing, they kind of explain what we did.

The text of these pages was written in 1994, after we returned from our expedition, and formed the report which we sent out to all our sponsors. I turned them into a web site because we couldn't afford to get anymore paper copies printed, mainly because the cost of having colour photos in the report was so high. Hopefully there will be a few people who are interested enough to have a look through some of these pages, but I will be the first to admit that some of them are heavy going, these pages are there for completeness and the people with a special interest in the work we did.
