MEDICAL REPORT

Thankfully our Expedition suffered no major medical problems. The most serious incident was when Jon slashed through a tree and into his knee with his bush knife.

(We just about managed to sew his leg back on ;-)

The most common problem during our time away was infected cuts and scratches. These were treated with iodine solution, and in more serious cases, aureomycin cream.

Much to our surprise no-one had any really bad problems with their digestive system. The biggest problem was adjusting to the diet as we changed from the bush to staying in peoples houses, and visa versa, the change in the richness and quantity of food was too much to cope with.
