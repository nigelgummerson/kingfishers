About Me

I am a student of Trinity College, Oxford. I am studying medicine at the John Radcliffe Hopsital, and will qualify as a Doctor in August 1997. As part of my final year I am going on elective (with Nerys Forester) to Australia where I will spend two weeks in Adelaide and seven weeks at the Gold Coast Medical center doing orthopaedics.

Along the way we are stopping in Hong Kong, Perth, Sydney, Auckland, Fiji, Los Angeles and Vancouver.
